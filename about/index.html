About

The Cards Chess Club strives to develop friendships, strengthen bonds, and most importantly have a good time! We exist not only for our love of chess, but for our strive to learn from each others' differences. I, Meli, as a prime representative of the Cards Chess Club, welcome anyone; regardless of background and skill, to join us and strive to be a better person, make cool new friends, and have fun along the way!

The Cards Chess Club was founded by Meli, alongside Gabe as not only a place for chess lovers to unite, but also as a place for people to connect.

Our mission as a club is to grow and create a community that both other people enjoy being in, and other people can connect in. If you want to join our community, see our Contact Us Page, and feel free to join our Discord!
